09 · Hidden Reservoir Realities
See What Others Miss — W_mod · TZ_pen · PHF
Three hidden factors that change every EOR recommendation and every NPV calculation
Wettability (W_mod)
Oil-wet rock has oil coating the pore walls. EOR methods that dislodge that film recover dramatically more oil than models without wettability correction predict. Derived from Amott-Harvey proxy using φ, Sw, and API gravity.
W_mod >1.10 → oil-wet 🟢 EOR amplified
0.90–1.10 → mixed-wet ⚪ neutral
<0.90 → water-wet 🟡 EOR reduced
Thief Zones (TZ_pen)
High-permeability streaks steal injected EOR fluid — polymer, CO₂, steam — away from the target oil zone. 80% of your spend can bypass the oil entirely. HyperSenseIQ detects them from the k/φ ratio diagnostic.
TZ_pen <0.20 → safe 🟢 proceed
0.20–0.50 → treat first 🟡
>0.50 → severe ⚠ UST only 🔴
P_EOR_adj = P_EOR × W_mod × (1 − TZ_pen × 0.15)
History Factor (PHF)
PHF anchors projections to what the well actually produced over 24 months — not what the model predicted. Enter real monthly data in Section 4 of Your Well Data. Everything adjusts silently — decline curve, EOR uplift, NPV.
PHF >1.05 → outperforming 🟢
0.92–1.05 → on track ⚪
<0.92 → investigate ⚠ skin?
